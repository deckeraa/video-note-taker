Family Memory Stream

Helping your family reminisce together

When you host your family videos with FamilyMemoryStream, your family can:

Stream videos to their computer or mobile device.

No need to pass around massive video files or DVDs.

Highlight their favorite memories by adding notes.

Notes can be added by any family member and correspond to a timestamp in a video, making it easy to jump to their favorite memories.

Find memories with built-in search.

Got a wedding coming up and looking for some cute/awkward baby videos? The search capability shows you all relevant clips.
